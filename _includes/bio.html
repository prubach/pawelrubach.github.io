Biography

I am a researcher as well as a practitioner. Throughout my career I have worked for or collaborated with several institutions. Universities: Warsaw School of Economics (my alma mater), Warsaw University of Technology, University of Warsaw, Texas Tech University, University of Virginia; NGOs: Stowarzyszenie Harcerskie, Stowarzyszenie 61, Vicariato San Jose del Amazonas); banks: PKO B.P.; telecom companies: Telekomunikacja Polska S.A., France Telecom R&D and government institutions (U.S. Air Force Research Laboratory).

My work focuses on developing and applying technology to various fields of science as well as everyday life. Earlier in my career, via the employment at TP S.A. and then my Fulbright scholarship at Texas Tech University I had been involved in working with engineers and computer scientists developing modeling environments that integrate engineering tools and applications, in particular, the SORCER project (http://sorcersoft.org/). Around 2018, I have shifted the main focus of my research and concentrated on applying distributed computing and Big Data to bioinformatics and structural biology. Since then, I closely collaborate with Joanna Sulkowska and her lab (Laboratory of Biological Systems Modeling) at the University of Warsaw as well as Wladek Minor and the Minorlab at the University of Virginia.

In my free time I love to travel and explore the world. I realize this collaborating with: ADVFactory.com (Krzysztof Samborski) and Przygody4x4
